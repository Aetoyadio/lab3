    International university of information technology

    IITU is the only university in Kazakhstan that received institutional international accreditation ASIIN in 2019 and passed the procedure of official confirmation of compliance with international standards.
    “The International University of Information Technology guarantees the fulfillment of institutional, procedural and cultural requirements for quality teaching and successful learning,” confirm experts from ASIIN (Agency for Accreditation of Educational Programs in Engineering, Computer Science, Natural Sciences and Mathematics, Germany).
    Over 10 years of development, IITU was able to firmly enter the ranking of the best technical universities in the country. The university is constantly working to improve the quality of educational services and ensures the high potential of its teaching staff.
    In December 2019, according to the rating of the National Chamber of Entrepreneurs “Atameken”, MUIT entered the TOP-3 universities in Kazakhstan, as well as first place in such educational programs as “information systems” and “electronic journalism”.
    The direction of training specialists in “information security” at IITU is one of the strongest in Kazakhstan.
    The modern infrastructure of the university, the harmonization of educational programs with leading international universities contribute to the dynamic growth of the student population. Currently, more than 4,000 students are studying at IITU in bachelor's, master's and PhD doctoral programs. The university is developing a new generation of specialists in the field of ICT with knowledge of industry technologies, advanced management, economics and communication skills with in-depth knowledge of the English language.
    In 2018, in accordance with the Order of the Minister of Defense of the Republic of Kazakhstan, a military department was opened at MUIT, specializing in the training of cybersecurity specialists, and a Cybersecurity Competence Center also operates.
    IITU's partners are international universities and scientific and educational organizations in the USA, Germany, Russia, France, Switzerland, Korea, Malaysia, China, etc.
    Existing academic mobility allows students to gain knowledge and international experience by studying for a semester at universities that are partners of the University.
    The University teachers are especially proud of the fact that after graduating from IITU, the percentage of graduates who are employed is always consistently high: about 90% of the total.
    IITU pays special attention to the development of personal qualities, leadership skills and creative abilities of students. Active and creative students independently conduct various events to make their student life bright and unforgettable, and lead projects that evoke admiration and surprise.
    There is a Student Government at IITU, which heads more than 20 student organizations, among them: “Union” - a charitable organization, “League of IT” - a football organization, “Tigers” - a creative organization, “ShadowS” - a dance club, “Music Club” - music club, "Debate Club" - debate club, "Enactus" - business organization, "IITU Family" - volunteer organization, "Anteiku" - anime club, "IT FM - student radio, "Cinema club" - film club, "IT Theater "- theater club, "Ray's Sup", "Light Studio" - media organizations, KVN team of MIIT, "FOREVER YOUNG" - Design, Art and Arts. We also have our own Ministry of Culture, Ministry of PR and Security Service.
